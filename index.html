About Us

Dugnad Primary School is a nurturing educational environment for children from Primary 1 to Primary 7. We focus on holistic development through play-based learning, academic excellence, and character building.

Our Philosophy

We believe every child is unique. Our child-centered approach ensures each pupil develops at their own pace while building confidence and love for learning.

Qualified Teachers

Our teachers are trained in primary education with special focus on early childhood development and primary school pedagogy.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we nurture social skills, emotional intelligence, creativity, and physical development through various activities.

Primary School Admissions

We welcome new pupils to join our Primary 1 class and consider transfers to other classes based on availability. Admission for Trinity Term 2026 is now open.

Admission Requirements

1

Age Requirement

Child must be at least 6 years old for Primary 1 admission.

2

Documents Needed

Birth certificate, immunization card, 2 passport photos, previous school report (for transfers).

3

Assessment

Basic literacy and numeracy assessment for P1 admissions.

4

Parent Interview

Meeting with school administration to discuss educational goals.
