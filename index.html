About Dugnad School

Our History

Founded in 1995, Dugnad School has been providing quality education for over 25 years, nurturing future leaders of Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops intellectual, physical, social, and spiritual potential of every learner.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, producing responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.
